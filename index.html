Our Values

The principles that shape how we work

Core value

We strive for excellence

Through our actions and behaviours, we aim for excellence in our interactions with clients, patients and colleagues.

Supporting behaviours

  • We take pride in our work and uphold high standards.
  • We are proactive, open to challenging the status quo and embracing change.
  • We view mistakes as learning opportunities and seek creative solutions.

Core value

We do the right thing

We are respectful and understanding of each other, and we treat others fairly and with honesty.

Supporting behaviours

  • We are respectful, compassionate, and supportive.
  • We build trust by being approachable, reliable, and consistent.
  • We base decisions on evidence and experience with the aim of being fair, ethical, and unbiased.

Core value

We are stronger together

We do our best work together, with each of us contributing our expertise and ideas to create better outcomes.

Supporting behaviours

  • We welcome feedback from colleagues and clients.
  • We are open to different perspectives and embrace diversity and the value it brings.
  • We take accountability for our roles and achieving shared goals.
  • We are honest and transparent, sharing information and giving constructive feedback.
